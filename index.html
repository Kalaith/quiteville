About This Game

Quiteville is a relaxing idle town builder where you restore a forgotten settlement, attracting new residents and watching them live their lives.

Start with a map of abandoned ruins and restore them into thriving community centers. Watch villagers move in, wander your town, go to work, shop, and socialize. Your town continues to grow even while you are away!

Controls

  • WASDPan Camera
  • ScrollZoom In / Out
  • ClickSelect Zone or Villager
  • BToggle Projects (Build) Menu
  • RToggle Research Tree
  • SpacePause / Resume
  • +/-Adjust Game Speed

Features

• Restoration Gameplay - Rebuild ruins into thriving centers
• Living Population - Villagers with needs (Hunger, Energy, Social)
• Idle Progression - Town grows while you're away
• Research Tree - Unlock new technologies
• Dynamic Economy - Balance Materials, Attractiveness, and Stability

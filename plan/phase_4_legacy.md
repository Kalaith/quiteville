# Phase 4: Legacy & Dynasty (Month 4)

**Goal**: Implementing the "Layer 3" long-term goals. Giving a reason to play for months.

## Week 13: Prestige & The Chronicle (TFL "Album")
*   [ ] **The Chronicle**: A permanent interactive book tracking all past towns and notable villagers.
*   [ ] **Villager Album**: "Collecting" villagers by recording their stories/feats before they pass away.
*   [ ] **Legacy Points**: Currency earned by filling the Chronicle, used for global upgrades.

## Week 14: Wonders & Great Projects
*   [ ] **Wonder Sites**: Special map nodes that require massive resource dumps.
*   [ ] **Construction Logic**: Multi-stage construction that spans multiple play sessions.
*   [ ] **Visual Payoff**: Dramatic changes to the world map upon completion.

## Week 15: Ancestors System
*   [ ] **Halls of Heroes**: Ability to "Retire" a favorite villager from a finished town.
*   [ ] **Ancestral Spirits**: Retired villagers appear in new towns as ghosts or statues providing buffs.
*   [ ] **Lineage UI**: Tracking the family trees of your best agents.

## Week 16: "The Cloud Spire" (Endgame Goal)
*   [ ] **The Final Project**: Implement the ultimate Wonder.
*   [ ] **Ending Sequence**: Narrative closure for completing the Spire.
*   [ ] **New Game+**: Resetting the universe with a "Constellation" modifier.
